Features

Everything your store needs

Built specifically for video game, comic, and collectible stores. Every feature designed around how you actually work.

💰
Point of Sale
Scan QR codes to instantly add items to cart. Accept cash, card, store credit, or split payments. Print thermal receipts with a scannable QR code for easy returns. Training mode for new staff.
🖥 POS screenshot coming soon
📊
Live Market Pricing
Pull real eBay completed sale prices for any game — loose, CIB, or sealed. Automatically calculates your cash and store credit trade-in offers.
📈 Pricing screenshot coming soon
🔄
Trade-In System
Look up market prices, build a trade-in quote, print an invoice, and add everything to inventory in one flow. Cash or store credit payout options.
🏷
SKU & Label Printing
Scan a UPC to auto-fill game details from IGDB. Generate SKU codes and print QR labels. Scan the label at the register to sell instantly.
↩
Returns & Exchanges
Scan the receipt QR to pull up original line items. Select items to return or exchange. Fraud protection prevents double-returns on the same receipt.
📦
Inventory Management
Full inventory with SKU tracking, cost vs. price, margin %, condition, channel, and notes. Sort, filter, search, and export to CSV.
💳
Loyalty & Store Credit
Customer profiles with points, store credit, visit history, and birthday tracking. Apply coupons and discounts at checkout.
📈
P&L & Reporting
Real-time profit & loss, expense tracking, shift reports, and transaction history. Know your numbers without a spreadsheet.
How it works

Up and running in a day

No hardware to buy, no IT department needed. If you can use a browser, you can run RetroVault.

1

Request a demo

Fill out the form below and we'll reach out personally — usually within 24 hours. We walk you through the system and answer any questions specific to your store.

2

Get your store set up

We create your account, configure your product types, tax rates, and printer settings. You get a welcome package with everything you need to get started.

3

Add your inventory

Scan UPC barcodes to auto-fill game details and generate SKU labels. Or import from a spreadsheet. Most stores are fully loaded within a day.

4

Open for business

Pull up the POS on any device — tablet, laptop, or desktop. Scan, sell, trade. Your data is in the cloud and always backed up.
